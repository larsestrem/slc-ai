**Effective date: July 19, 2026**

This policy explains what information senior.living.community (the "Site") collects, how we
use it, and the choices you have. The short version: **the Site is built to collect as little
as possible.** You can read every directory listing and every guide without creating an
account, without being tracked by us, and without telling us anything.

## What we collect

**Nothing, for browsing.** There is no account system, no newsletter, no login, and we do not
run analytics or advertising trackers. Our cost calculator and worksheets run entirely in
your browser - nothing you type into them is sent to us or anyone else.

**What you choose to send us:**

- **Community questionnaires and listing claims.** If you represent a senior living community
  and submit a questionnaire or claim a listing, we collect what you provide: your name, job
  title, work email, your community's identity, and your answers. We use it to verify you and
  to build your community's listing. Published answers are attributed on the listing with
  your name, title, and submission date - that attribution is part of what you agree to in
  our [Terms](/terms/#5-community-provided-content-and-listing-terms).
- **Email you send us.** If you email us - a correction, a question, a claim request - we
  receive your address and whatever you include, and we use it to respond and to fix what
  you reported.

**Collected automatically, by infrastructure:** our hosting and content-delivery providers
keep standard server logs (IP address, pages requested, browser type) to operate and secure
the service, as essentially every website's infrastructure does. We do not use these logs to
identify or profile visitors.

## How form submissions travel

Questionnaire forms on the Site are delivered to us by a third-party form-forwarding service
(FormSubmit), which transmits your submission to our private email and is not permitted to
use it for its own purposes. Submissions are **private** - they go to us alone, and nothing
you submit is published until we have verified you and prepared the listing content with you.

## How we use information

- To verify that a person claiming a community listing actually represents that community
  (including checking the state license record and calling the community's published number)
- To build, correct, and attribute community listings
- To respond to questions and correction reports
- To operate and secure the Site

That's the whole list. **We do not sell or rent personal information - to anyone, ever** - and we do not share it with listed communities or advertisers, run marketing lists, or use
submitted information for any purpose other than the ones above.

## Sharing

We share information only with: (a) service providers who help operate the Site (hosting,
form delivery, email), who may use it solely to provide the service; (b) authorities, if a
valid legal process requires it; and (c) a successor operator if the Site changes hands, in
which case this policy continues to apply to already-collected information.

## Retention and deletion

We keep community submissions while the listing is live so we can attribute and re-verify
content, and correspondence for as long as it's useful for running the Site. You can ask us
at any time to correct or delete information you sent us, including removing your name from
a listing's attribution - email **listings@senior.living.community** and we'll confirm when
it's done.

## Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live (including California and other states with consumer privacy
laws), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information, and to know
what has been collected about you. Since we collect only what this policy describes, the
answer will be short - but we honor these requests regardless of where you live. Email
**listings@senior.living.community**. We do not sell or share personal information as those
terms are defined by those laws, so there is nothing to opt out of. We do not discriminate
against anyone for exercising a privacy right.

## Children

The Site is written for adults researching senior care and is not directed to children. We
do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 16; if you believe a child sent us
information, contact us and we will delete it.

## Third-party sites

Links on the Site - government databases, review platforms, community websites - lead to
sites with their own privacy practices, which we do not control. What you do there is
governed by their policies, not this one.

## Security

Submissions travel over encrypted connections (HTTPS), and access to what you send us is
limited to the people who run the Site. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly
secure, but there is also very little here to breach: we hold no accounts, no passwords, and
no payment information.

## Changes and contact

If this policy changes, we'll update it here with a new effective date; material changes will
be noted on this page. Questions or requests: **listings@senior.living.community**.
