{%- comment -%} One-line legend for the star ratings on the community cards below, so the stars on a listing are never read without knowing what they are or where they came from. {%- endcomment -%}

About the stars: on a nursing home, ★ is its Medicare government rating (from inspections and staffing); elsewhere, ★ is a family-review average (read it with care). Each community's page shows the rating with its source, count, and date.
