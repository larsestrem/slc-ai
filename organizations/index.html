Senior Living Organizations

Many communities belong to multi-state operators. Company policy shapes pricing, staffing, and care assessments - but quality still varies building to building, so judge the building your parent would live in, not the brand: check its own state record and its own reviews.

We show two numbers for each operator: roughly how many communities it runs nationwide, and how many are in the states we currently cover (Oregon, Washington, Idaho, and Hawaii). Nationwide counts are approximate and change as companies buy and sell buildings. As we add states, the second number grows. Operators with no community in a state we cover yet don't appear here.
