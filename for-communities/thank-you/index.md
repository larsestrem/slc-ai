**Your answers are on their way to our editors - privately.** Nothing you sent is published
automatically: we'll verify you with your community first (a quick call to your published
main number), prepare your listing content, and follow up by email before anything goes live.

## What happens next

1. **Verification** - we confirm your role with your community and check the state license
   record. This usually takes a few business days.
2. **We prepare your listing** and email you what we plan to publish, attributed to you by
   name, title, and date.
3. **You confirm or correct**, and it goes live.
