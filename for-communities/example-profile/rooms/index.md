## What to confirm on your tour

Floor plans are a starting point - square footage and layouts vary by wing and building year.
When you visit any community, ask to see the *actual* apartment your parent would move into, not
the model. Check our [touring checklist](/guides/touring-checklist/) and
[what's not included in the rate](/guides/whats-not-included-hidden-fees/) - in most communities
the apartment type sets the base rent, and care charges are added on top.
