Community Comparison Worksheet

The trap in comparing communities is that each one quotes differently. This worksheet fixes that: the same questions, asked everywhere, side by side. Fill it in on screen as you call and tour, or and bring it with you.

Your answers stay on your device only - nothing you type here is sent or saved anywhere. Print or screenshot when you're done.
