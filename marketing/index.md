We run senior.living.community, the directory families use to research senior care — which
means we spend every day watching how families actually search, what they click, and what
makes them pick up the phone. This page is for the people on the other side of that search:
the communities.

Most senior living communities can't justify a dedicated digital marketer. So marketing gets
done in the gaps — a brochure refresh here, a boosted Facebook post there, and a referral-
platform invoice for **$3,000–6,000+** every time a bed fills. There's a better way to spend
that money, and it isn't complicated: reach the families who are *already searching* for
exactly what you offer, in your city, this week — and give them a website that answers their
questions instead of hiding the ball.

## What full occupancy actually buys you

An empty bed at $6,000 a month is roughly **$200 a day** that never comes back. But the
bigger prize isn't filling this month's vacancy — it's demand that consistently exceeds your
capacity. A community with a waitlist doesn't market from desperation. It admits the
residents it can serve best, families start their relationship with confidence instead of a
hard sell, reviews improve because fit improves, and those reviews bring the next wave of
families. Quality and occupancy aren't competing goals; done right, each one feeds the other.

That flywheel is what we build marketing around — not clicks for their own sake.

## The programs

**[Ad Grant Management](/marketing/ad-grants/)** — for nonprofit communities. Google gives
eligible 501(c)(3) organizations **$10,000/month** in donated search advertising (Microsoft
adds ~**$3,000/month** more), and most nonprofit communities never collect a dollar of it.
We secure the grant and run it: **$2,000/month, 6-month minimum**. If we can't get your grant
approved, you owe nothing. If your campaigns don't reach the traffic minimum, the setup fee
comes back. For-profit communities get the same management on a conventional ad budget.

**[Website Optimization](/marketing/website-optimization/)** — traffic only matters if it
converts. We audit and fix the things that quietly cost you tours: slow pages, confusing
care-level language, pricing walls, buried contact paths, missing trust signals.

**[Website Rebuild](/marketing/website-rebuild/)** — when the current site can't be saved
economically, we build from the ground up: fast, clear, structured for both search engines
and the AI assistants families increasingly ask first. Optimization designed in beats
optimization bolted on.

**[Free: what actually works](/marketing/best-practices/)** — our honest playbook on
channels, reviews, reputation, and training your own tour team. No charge, no email wall.
If you only read one page, read that one. And see
**[the cost comparison](/marketing/cost-comparison/)** for what a targeted visitor costs
next to TV, radio, mail, and per-placement referral fees.
