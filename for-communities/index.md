Families use this directory to find and shortlist communities. Accurate listings help them —
and you. Claiming and updating a listing is free.

## Update or claim a listing

Email **listings@senior.living.community** from an address at your community's domain with:

1. The community's name and the page URL on this site (or "new listing")
2. What needs to change — contact details, care levels, capacity, description, website
3. Your name and role at the community

We verify every request against your state license record before publishing changes, and we
never remove links to official inspection records — families rely on them, and honest
communities benefit from them.

## Add a new community

Not listed yet? Send the same email with your community's name, address, license number, care
levels offered, and website. Licensed communities in our covered states are listed at no charge.

## A note on how we work

We're independent. We don't charge for placement referrals and don't accept payment to rank one
community above another. Enhanced listing options (photos, expanded descriptions) may be offered
in the future — but the directory itself stays free and factual.
