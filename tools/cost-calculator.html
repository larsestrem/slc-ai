How to read these numbers

The cheapest line isn't automatically the right one. A few hours of in-home care suits lower needs and a safe home; around-the-clock home care usually costs more than assisted living — and isolation at home carries its own cost. Small care homes are often the value surprise. When you're comparing real communities, remember the advertised rate isn't the real rate: read what's not included and home care or a facility? — then browse communities near your family.
