About 5–10 minutes. Reply to our email (or write **listings@senior.living.community**) with
your community name and the numbered answers — skip anything that doesn't apply or that you'd
rather not publish. Partial answers are welcome; you can always add more later.

## The community

1. Official licensed name (and the name families know you by, if different)
2. Year opened, and year of the most recent major renovation
3. Ownership: independent, family-owned, non-profit, or part of an organization (which one?)
4. State license number(s) and license type(s)

## Capacity & rooms

5. Licensed capacity (beds/residents)
6. Current approximate resident count
7. Room types offered: private rooms, shared/companion rooms, studios, one-bedrooms, two-bedrooms, cottages
8. Approximate square footage of your most common room type(s)
9. Do rooms have private bathrooms? Kitchenettes?

## Building & grounds

10. Total building square footage (approximate is fine)
11. Number of floors; elevator available?
12. Outdoor spaces: courtyard, gardens, walking paths, secured outdoor area for memory care

## Staffing

13. Total staff count (approximate)
14. Caregiving staff on duty during the day vs overnight (families ask this constantly)
15. Is a nurse on site 24/7, on site during business hours, or on call? (RN or LPN?)
