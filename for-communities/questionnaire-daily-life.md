About 5–10 minutes — and honestly the section families enjoy reading most. Reply to our email
(or write **listings@senior.living.community**) with your community name and the numbered
answers. Partial answers are welcome.

## Dining

1. Dining style: restaurant-style, buffet/cafeteria, set seatings, open hours?
2. Is room delivery available, and is there a charge?
3. How many meals per day are included? Snacks?
4. Special diets you accommodate (diabetic, low-sodium, pureed, vegetarian, kosher/halal)
5. Can families join for meals, and what does a guest meal cost?

## Activities & engagement

6. A typical week: what's on the calendar? (Feel free to attach a real recent activity calendar — families love seeing the actual thing.)
7. Physical fitness offerings (exercise classes, gym equipment, walking groups, PT-led programs)
8. Games, clubs, and social events (cards, bingo, book clubs, happy hours, live music, holiday events)
9. Entertainment and enrichment (performers, lectures, crafts, gardening, intergenerational programs)
10. Religious/spiritual services on site or nearby
11. For memory care: what does engagement look like on your secured unit specifically?

## Getting out & around

12. Scheduled transportation: where does the shuttle go, and how often (shopping, errands, outings)?
13. Medical appointment transportation: available? scheduled how far ahead? any fee?
14. Group outings: how often, and where have you gone recently?
15. Visiting hours and overnight guest policy

Done with all three? Wonderful — you've given families a real picture of your community.
